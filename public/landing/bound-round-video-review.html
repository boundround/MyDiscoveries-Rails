HOW TO MAKE A STRESS FREE
Bound Round Video Review

Before you leave home

1.   Chose an attraction to review, set a date & get
      pschyed!

2.   Get a video camera, smart phone or Go Pro with a
      FULLY CHARGED battery!

3.   Ask your mum, dad, big brother or sister to be in
      charge of the filming.

4.  Ask a friend or sibling to come; it’s more fun with
     your squad!

DON'T STRESS - it's just another cheesey family video!

When you get there

1.  Always hold your camera sideways & hold every
     shot for 10 seconds.

2.  Film a wide shot of the outside of the venue.

3.  If you can also film a sign or company logo that
     would be great!

4.  Get your reporters standing outside & keep your shot
     steady.

5.  Get them to smile or wave before heading inside. 

6.  Together choose 3 elements of your day to feature.

3-2-1 ACTION!

1.  For each activity film at least FOUR shots of what’s
     going on.

2.  Hold the camera still and if you need to re-frame,
     reposition yourself. Don't zoom while filming!

3.  A wide shot (inside or out) is a great way to set the
     scene. 

4.  Vary your shots - close ups, far away, even faces!

5.  Show others the best things about what you’ve done.

6.  Don’t worry about the sound - the pictures will tell
     the story.

7.  Waterproof cameras?  SURE! Selfie sticks?  WHY NOT! 
     Be creative!

AT THE END
Get your reporters to rate their visit on paper & hold it to the camera

Back at home

1.  Find a quiet spot inside or out & get your reporter to
     recount their day.

2.  Workshop what they’re going to say before you press
     record.

3.  Ideally, get them to answer the standard questions:
             Hi, my name is...
             Today I went to...
             First we did...
             I liked it because...
             Then we... etc.
             Finally we went to...
             Overall the place was great because...

4.  Try your hand at editing your footage into a short
     completed video (under 2 minutes) - iMovie is simple and
     easy! Then upload to Bound Round’s YouTube channel
     (we’ll send you the details).

5.  If editing is too hard, we’ll send you a USB to save your
     footage onto and our editors will weave their magic & your
     footage into a video
